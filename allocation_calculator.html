📖 Why Two Buckets at This Fund Size

1
CC requires 100 shares minimum per contract. At <$150K, the Wheel bucket would hold too few shares to sell even one CC. Forcing it wastes capital that should be compounding.
2
BPS generates income without owning shares. Your 20% BPS collateral earns premium every 45-day cycle while 80% of capital compounds in Core. Same income goal, no share count requirement.
3
Build Wheel shares via put assignment (Mode 2). Run sell puts at your target dip prices. Every assignment adds shares to a future Wheel bucket — organically, at good prices.
4
All premium reinvests into Core. BPS income + any put premium → buy more SPY/QQQ Core shares each cycle. This is the compounding accelerator.
