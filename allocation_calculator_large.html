📖 Why These Allocations

1
Core is your wealth engine (default 70%). Long-term compounding does the heavy lifting. Options income is supplementary — never let it crowd out Core.
2
Wheel bucket (default 15%) generates CC income. At this fund size you have enough shares for 1+ CC contracts per ticker. Each assignment feeds cash back into the put-selling cycle.
3
BPS collateral (default 15%) earns income without owning shares. Runs in parallel with everything else. All premium reinvests into Core.
4
55/45 SPY/QQQ split. SPY provides stability. QQQ overweighted for growth. Adjust the slider based on your risk tolerance.
5
All options income reinvests into Core. $800/month premium compounding at 10% annual return over 20 years = meaningful additional wealth. The premium is not the wealth — the compounded shares are.
